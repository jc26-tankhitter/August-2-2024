Giants are better than the Dodgers

by Josiah Chang

To begin, Blake Snell, as of August 2, 2024 has just thrown a no hitter agianst the Cincinatti Reds. He recorded 11 strikeouts and walked only 3. He recently also struck out 15 hitters in his last start.

Furthermore, the Giants' rookie relief pitcher, Erik Miller, has struck out Dodgers star Shohei Ohtani 5 times in 5 at-bats.

While some may argue that the Dodgers have the better record and have better players, the Giants have been excellent while playing from behind, most notably against the Pittsburgh Pirates and overcoming grand slam deficits.

Finally, the Giants are the best team in baseball because of their pitching and resillience. BEAT LA!!!!!!
